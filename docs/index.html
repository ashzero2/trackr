Privacy Policy for Trackr

Last updated: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information we process and how we handle it.

Trackr primarily stores your financial data on your device. This can include transactions, categories, budgets, and profile preferences that you enter in the app.

Data is stored locally on your device (for example, using local database and app storage). We do not require account creation to use core features.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your financial records with third parties except when required by law.

4. Data Export and Backup

The app may provide export/import features (such as CSV or JSON) so you can manage your own backups. You are responsible for protecting exported files.

5. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect data handled by the app. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Trackr is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
